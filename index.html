About us

The Pine-Richland School District takes pride in its technology and engineering education program. Focused on providing students with technical skill sets for college, trade school, or the work force, Pine-Richland offers a wide variety of classes to give students the opportunity to acquire these skills. With our high grade and industrial equipment, it will teach and prepare students to use the latest in today's world.
